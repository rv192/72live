1.  [沙特阿拉伯在FII9峰会推出SAGE人工智能计划 - Peter H. Diamandis, MD](https://x.com/PeterDiamandis/status/1984031235832992174)

2.  [Baseten Training 正式发布，提供高级模型训练服务 - sarah guo](https://x.com/saranormous/status/1984028331084542131)
