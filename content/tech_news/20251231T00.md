3.  [吴恩达分享AI系统构建者的职业发展建议-Andrew Ng](https://x.com/AndrewYNg/status/2005702832524255475)

4.  [Claude Pro/Max用户可免API Key进行本地Agent SDK开发-宝玉](https://x.com/dotey/status/2005714387060023749)
